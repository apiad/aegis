# The Meaning of Life

The meaning of life is a profound philosophical question that has puzzled humanity throughout history. Many believe it is to find your purpose, to seek knowledge, to experience joy, to create or connect with something greater than yourself. Ultimately, each person must find their own meaning through relationships, work, and personal growth.
